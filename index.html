Join the Holochain Sprint-athon 29 Jan - 4 Feb at Newspeak House. Holochain is a scalable post blockchain decentralised computing platform with versatile crypto-backed public/private validation, with data shared across a network of full peers.

Holo's ethical crowdfunding programme has just entered the ICO phase.Some of the main players in the Holo team will be with us in London for the full week. Meet Holo’s primary architect Arthur Brock, who will be on hand to help translate your ideas to a distributed platform implementation. Connor Turland will also be on site, who brings his experience with building metamaps and other data visualzation systems to help you build an interface worth having.

Come along to code, to hear from them what it's all about, to engage in the wider discussions around related issues that darVOZ will be facilitating during the week.The Sprint-athon will focus principally on bringing Liquid Wiki to Holochain, with work on Mutual Credit systems and Argument Mapping also underway.You are welcome, of course, to bring your own project, or simply come and learn about Holochain.
