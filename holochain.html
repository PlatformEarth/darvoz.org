A HOLOCHAIN SPRINT-ATHON

#darVOZweek 2018
29 Jan - 4 Feb 

Scalable, P2P post-blockchain

Holochain is a scalable post blockchain decentralised computing platform with versatile crypto-backed public/private validation, with data sharded across a network of full peers.
Holo's ethical crowdfunding programme has just entered the ICO phase.
Some of the main players in the Holo team will be with us in London for the full week.
Come along to code, to hear from them what it's all about, to engage in the wider discussions around related issues that darVOZ will be facilitating during the week.
The Sprint-athon will focus principally on bringing Liquidwiki to Holochain, with work on Mutual Credit systems and Argument Mapping also underway.
You are welcome, of course, to bring your own project, or simply come and learn about Holochain.
